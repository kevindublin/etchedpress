Bright Burning Fuse Review

Melanie Faith's Bright, Burning Fuse has a review in the latest issue of the Wild Goose Poetry Review. The poems are said to effectively capture "the ambiance of late 20th century rural America using the only two tools available to the poet, language and imagery." Remember to read these "appealing" poems and the rest of the review, here.
